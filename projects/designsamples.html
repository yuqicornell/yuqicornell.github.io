Twitter Tab Bar Redesign

  • This idea came from when I was using many mobile applications with one hand on the subway.
  • I found that using the tab bar with one hand was a pain. For me, a right-handed person, the leftmost tab-bar icon was often difficult to press. Yes, I was able to reach it, but it forced me to use a very uncomfortable hand gesture.
  • That was why I wanted to redesign the Tab Bar. To illustrate one design idea, I also created an interactive prototype (Origami)
